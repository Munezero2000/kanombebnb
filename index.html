Welcome to Kanombe

Kanombe is a suburb in Kigali, Rwanda and has an elevation of 1,421 metres. Kanombe is situated nearby to the suburbs Free Trade Zone and Kimironko. Into guide you will be provided with information on various essential facilities in Kanombe, including Kigali International Airport, schools, hospitals, police stations, and petrol stations. Whether you are a resident or a visitor, this website can assist you in finding the necessary information about these key locations in kanombe.

Services

Kigali International Airport

The airport is located in the suburb of Kanombe Sector, at the eastern edge of Kigali, approximately 5 kilometres (3.1 mi), by road, east of the central business district of the city of Kigali

Rwanda Military Hospital

The Government of Rwanda, through the Ministries of Health and Defense, is committed to offering quality health care services to the military and the general population through strengthening and refocusing RMH

Hilltop Hotel and Country Club

Located in Kigali, 1Kilometer from Kigali international Airport, Hilltop Hotel features a terrace, a shared lounge, a bar and free WiFi access. This 3-star hotel has a 24-hour reception.

Nyandungu Eco-Park

The Nyandungu Eco-Park provides a space for residents and visitors to the city to explore and learn from nature, and is part of Rwanda’s efforts to harness eco-tourism to restore biodiversity.

Presidential place museum

This place is a house that the former presidents of the country used to reside and it is currently a museum that both the citizens and the visitor can visit

King David Academy

King David Academy is located in Kigali – Rwanda, along Kanombe Airport Road. The school offers both national and Cambridge curriculums with a variety of co-curricular activities like Music, Dance, Drama, Sports etc

Kanombe SDA English Church

The church is located along the Airport Road 3km away from the airport. They have a service every saturday and Everyone is welcomed in the presence of God

Rwanda National Police

Rwanda National Police officers and employees work tirelessly with courage and dedication to deliver essential policing services respectfully and professionally, be there for you in emergencies, and ensure that your environment is free of crime ...
